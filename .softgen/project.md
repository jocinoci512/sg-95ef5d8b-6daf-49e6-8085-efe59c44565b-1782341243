# Enterprise Logistics Platform

## Vision
Complete logistics and vehicle transportation platform for Go Cargo Logistics — freight terminal command center aesthetic with professional tracking, customer management, and operations dashboard.

## Design
**Industrial Command Center** — freight dispatch terminals, utilitarian precision, dark slate with amber alerts.

Colors:
- `--background: 210 40% 10%` (deep slate #0F172A)
- `--foreground: 210 20% 90%` (steel-gray #E2E8F0)
- `--primary: 38 92% 50%` (amber #F59E0B)
- `--accent: 27 96% 61%` (warm amber #FB923C)
- `--muted: 215 25% 27%` (graphite #334155)
- `--card: 217 33% 17%` (elevated slate #1E293B)
- `--border: 215 20% 25%` (subtle #374151)

Typography:
- Headings: Space Mono (monospace command)
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (technical clarity)
- Data: JetBrains Mono (tabular precision)

Style: High-contrast data hierarchy, monospace grids, amber action hits, generous spacing around data clusters, terminal-inspired UI.

## Features
**Public Site**: Hero tracking, services, about, quote requests, blog, contact
**Customer Portal**: Auth, dashboard, shipment tracking, history, documents, support
**Admin Dashboard**: Shipment management, customer management, quotes, analytics, content/blog management
**Database**: Customers, shipments, quotes, tracking events, blog posts, testimonials, inquiries
